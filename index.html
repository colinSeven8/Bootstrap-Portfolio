About Me

Greetings, humans. I know that this old high school picture of me probably looks like I'm saying, "hey, dude", but I'm really not. Back then, having bleached hair and sideburns was a thing - not so much now.

Some of my interests include artwork, soccer, biking, skateboarding, snowboarding, running around in the mountains, and melting faces with my guitar. I was introduced to Web development a couple of years ago and decided this is the way to go. I'm really excited to finally get the formal training I've wanted.
